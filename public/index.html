Team Snapshot

Griffins 1884 is one of ASL’s two FRC teams. The Blue Alliance lists the team out of London with a rookie year of 2006 and a long award record that includes FIRST Impact, regional wins, Woodie Flowers Finalist, Sustainability, Engineering Inspiration, and design awards.

In recent seasons, the team has added a 2026 regional win in Brazil, a 2026 FIRST Impact Award, a 2025 Sustainability Award, and a 2023 Engineering Inspiration Award. The program also shares its school base with sister team 1797 Phoenix, another long-running ASL FRC team.
