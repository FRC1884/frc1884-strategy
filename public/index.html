Griffins 1884

Griffins 1884 is the American School in London’s FIRST Robotics Competition team, competing out of London since 2006. Recent public results include the 2026 Brazil regional win, the 2026 Regional FIRST Impact Award, and earlier regional wins in Shenzhen and New York City.

Team Identity

Griffins 1884 is one of ASL’s FRC teams and has built a competition record that includes regional wins, FIRST Impact, Sustainability, Engineering Inspiration, and Woodie Flowers Finalist honors.

The team’s public identity is rooted in FRC competition: designing, building, traveling, scouting, and preparing for match play at regional and championship events.
